One-star

Inviting 3 first-level friends to join and purchase products worth 1300rs or more will make you a One-Star Member. You will receive a monthly salary of 500rs and a one-time bonus of 300rs.

Two-star

Inviting 5 first-level friends to join and purchase products worth 1300rs or more will make you a Two-Star Member. You will receive a monthly salary of 1000rs and a one-time bonus of 500rs.

Three-star

Inviting 8 first-level friends to join and purchase products worth 1300rs or more will make you a Three-Star Member. You will receive a monthly salary of 1500rs and a one-time bonus of 800rs.

Four-star

Inviting 12 first-level friends to join and purchase products worth 1300rs or more, and among these 12, having 1 person become a Three-Star Member will make you a Four-Star Member. You will receive a monthly salary of 3000rs and a one-time bonus of 1300rs.

Five-star

Inviting 15 first-level friends to join and purchase products worth 1300rs or more, and among these 15, having 3 people become Three-Star Members will make you a Five-Star Member. You will receive a monthly salary of 6000rs and a one-time bonus of 2000rs.

Commission: Bain Communityâ€™s commission is divided into two levels, the first level commission is 7% and the second level commission is 3%.

The friends you invite are A-level friends. After A-level friends purchase products, you will receive a commission of 7% of their consumption. For example: you invite 5 A-level friends, and each of them purchases 5,000 products; The commission you get is: 5000*7%*5=1750; the commission will be credited to your account in real time.

Friends invited by A-level friends continue to invite others to join. These are your B-level friends. If there are 5 B-level friends, they all purchase 5,000 products. The B-level commission you receive is: 5000*3%*5=750.

Dividends: Your dividend rate is 3%. When friends you invite buy products, you can get 3% of your daily income as dividends. Every time a friend you invite receives their income, you'll receive dividends.

For example: If you invite 10 A-level friends and they all purchase 13,000 products, the product cycle of 13,000 is 270 days, and the daily income is 156rs; The daily dividend you get is: 156*3%*10=46.8rs. The total dividend received is: 46.8*270=12636rs.
